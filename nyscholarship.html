    New York Women in Communications Scholarships(NYWICI)

    Every year, the New York Women in Communications scholarship is awarded to 15 to 20 graduating high school seniors and graduate students who want to have a career in communications. These scholarhips range from $2,500-$10,000 for each female undergraduate and post-graduate student. New York Women in Communications include scholarships like the Heart Scholarship for women who want to pursue a career in the magazine industry. There's also the Esperanza Scholarship which supports Hispanic women who wants a media profession. Learn more about this oppurtunity at the New York Communications!

    Quest For Excellence Award: NYC Award

    The Quest for Excellence NYC Award is given to those who are high-achieving and low-income high school juniors in NYC. The NYC Award awards $100 to the participant. This award is elligible to Questbridge College Prep Scholars. This award is made possible through the great genorosity of the Heckscher Foundation and many other foundations and individuals. The Quest for Excellence gives out many more awards varied by different categories, so please take this oppurtunities and reach for success! Learn more information at Quest Bridge.

    Ascend Educational Fund Scholarships(AEF)

    The Ascend Educational Fund award scholarships are given to those who are immigrant students and are children of immigrants who are graduating from a NYC high school. This scholarship ranges from $2,500-$20,000 and helps to attend any college or university of their choosing no matter what their ethnicity, national origin, or immigration status is. Everyone deserves the education they need to reach for success and strive for your highest potential! Take advantage of this oppurtunity and learn more about it at Ascend Educational Fund
