    What is a Linkedin?

    Linkedin is a social platform for people to network and find jobs. There are millions of users and it's free to create a profile. You can purchase a premium account if you choose to.

    Why does it matter?

    Networking is imperative to finding a job and an online platform is an asset to you. It's a professional medium that you can use to keep in touch with others. At a click of a button you can find valuable information about your peers and potential employers.

    Tips & Tricks:

    1) Have a professional headshot for your profile.

    2) Have a professional email and contact information.

    3) Keep your experiences short and concise to keep the reader's attention.

    4) Constantly befriend and add the people you meet on this application.

    5) If you have a long description of your experience, use bulletpoints!
