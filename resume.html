    What is a resume?

    A resume is an overview of your experiences and qualifications that one is usually required to submit when applying for a job.

    Resume Examples:

    Why does it matter?

    A resume is a marketing tool! Your resume is the first step before deciding if you are qualified for an interview. Studies show that 8 out of 10 resumes are discarded with only a 10 second glance by an employer. (1)

    Tips & Tricks:

    1) Include sections for Contact Information, Education, Experience, Leadership & Activities and Skills & Interest.

    2) Search up resume examples and templates. There are free templates offered on Google, Microsoft and other sites.

    3) Try to keep your resume to your page. The employer's time is valuable, don't waste it!

    4) Consistently update your contact information.
